A little background

A group of girls has taken a series of craft classes. In the class, they have baked plain cupcakes and decorated their cakes with icings. Their parents would like to learn more about their activities. You have been hired as an assistant. Your task is to visit the girls after their class and record how many cupcakes they iced.

To do so, you simply need to look at a picture and select a sentence that adequately describes the situation.
